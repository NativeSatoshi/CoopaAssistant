We're often asked: "There are already dozens of AI assistants why build another one?"

Our answer is simple: Because existing AI is a visitor. We want to build the homeowner.

Most AI today forgets you the moment you close the browser tab. Your data sits trapped on corporate servers. Privacy hangs by a thread. And when it comes to real-world actions adding a calendar event, analyzing live market data, sending a contextual email they're still clumsy.

Coopa was born from that frustration. We want an AI that remembers the note you mentioned last week, thinks about your budget, builds bridges across languages, organizes your Google events based on your habits and most importantly, never forgets you.

The driving force behind this project isn't traditional code it's Gemini API's reasoning power and autonomous AI agents. We positioned Gemini not as a simple Q&A bot, but as the founding partner and brain of the entire system. It searches the web, interprets market data in seconds, organizes your calendar, communicates with your wallet. A tireless intelligence network, working for you in the background.
